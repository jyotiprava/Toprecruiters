What We Do

You want to stay in control of your wedding but there is a LOT that goes into planning a wedding that can be daunting and holy-crap-overwhelming. Wedding Penguine provides a blend of wedding coordination, consulting, and day-of management services to stellar couples. We want to guide you through logistics, walk you through to-do lists that keep you on-track, and introduce you to the best vendors we know. On your wedding day, we oversee setup and work with your vendors so you can relax and enjoy your day.

How We Can Help: Day-Of Coordination, Full Planning and Anything In Between.
