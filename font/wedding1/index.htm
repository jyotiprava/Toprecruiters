  • What We Do

    You want to stay in control of your wedding but there is a LOT that goes into planning a wedding that can be daunting and holy-crap-overwhelming.Wedding Penguine provides a blend of wedding coordination, consulting, and day-of management services to stellar couples.

  • Why We love It

    We love weddings. Seriously, we do. But we look at them from a logistical standpoint as well as from your guest's point-of-view. We believe in practical budgets with non-tacky splashes of personality and whimsy. Nothing pleases us more than seeing a couple enjoy their wedding day instead of worrying about what's going on behind the scenes.
